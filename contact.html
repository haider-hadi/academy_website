Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for a course?

You can sign up through our website by filling out the enrollment form. Our admissions team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your enrollment and discuss your learning goals.

What are your payment options?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as electronic bank transfers. We also offer flexible payment plans for longer courses.

Can I schedule sessions at my convenience?

Yes. We offer scheduled in-person classes and one-on-one sessions; you can book slots during our office hours to suit your timetable.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your first session, we'll provide a full refund.

How are lessons conducted?

All lessons are conducted in-person at our academy. Students attend classes on-site where tutors provide hands-on instruction and use classroom materials for effective learning.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can cancel or reschedule sessions up to 24 hours in advance without any penalties. We're flexible to accommodate your schedule.
